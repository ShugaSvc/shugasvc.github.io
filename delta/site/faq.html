Shuga is a reward with monetary value.

Yes! Real money, 200 Shuga is valued at SGD$1.00 and can be used to redeem store gift vouchers & products you know and love! You can explore in our app the full list of rewards!

1. Earn Shuga by simply walking into a partner store. They’re all listed on our application in order of proximity; you can also view our map to see what stores are offering Shuga near you!

2. Earn by scanning the barcode of products listed under ‘ Scan for Shuga’ inside our partner stores.

Once you find the item in the store and you are ready to scan, tap the item in the scan list. By clicking ‘Scan and Earn Shuga Now’ your phone’s camera will activate and you’ll see a white box with a pink line running through the middle. Line up the barcode with the box and pink line, and you’ll receive Shuga.

3. Earn Shuga as a purchase rebate from snapping partner store receipts.

You can submit receipts up to 24hours after the purchase is made, submit by opening the yellow receipt button on the bottom right-hand side, your phone’s camera will activate. Snap a nice clear picture and once submitted, it will go through a verification process.
