Want to Be Rewarded for Your Everyday Shopping?

Shuga is designed to do exactly so! Earn Shuga when you interact with Shuga-friendly stores.

#1 Visit Stores

Love exploring new stores? Earn Walk-in Shuga just by visiting our partner stores!

#2 Scanning Products

Fancy a treasure hunt in stores? Receive Scan-for-Shuga points by scanning featured product barcodes!

Want more bang for your buck? Simply snap your receipt from our partner stores to earn Shuga rebates!
